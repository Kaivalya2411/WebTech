Biryani

@ Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • Water
  • Basmati Rice
  • Oil
  • Biryani Masalas
  • Vegetables

@ Procedure

  1. Step 1 : Marinate the chicken: Mix chicken pieces with yogurt, spices (like turmeric, cumin, garam masala), ginger, and garlic. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Step 2 : Prepare rice: Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Boil water with whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves). Add rice and cook until it's 70% cooked. Drain.

  3. Step 3 : Sauté onions: In a large pot, sauté sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for later use.

  4. Step 4 : Layering: In the same pot, layer half the partially cooked rice, then the marinated chicken, chopped mint and cilantro, and fried onions. Repeat with the remaining rice, then fried onions.

  5. Step 5 : Seal the pot: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or dough to trap steam. Cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes.

  6. Step 6 : Final touches: Once done, gently fluff the biryani with a fork. Mix the layers slightly before serving to combine flavors.

  7. Step 7 : Serve: Serve the chicken biryani hot with raita, salad, or chutney.
